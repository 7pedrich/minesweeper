Minesweeper game I made in the holidays for fun. Feel free to do whatever you want to it, fork it, clone it, blah blah blah bleh bleh bleh
(You'll need sound effects in SoundService, but you can easily find these in the toolbox. Or maybe you can just delete the sound effect feature.)

Touch with your character to Reveal cell 

RMB to Toggle flag on/off
